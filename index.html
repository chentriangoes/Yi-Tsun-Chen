    Interest

    I am an independent scholar intersted in exploring how the digital development including the blockchain technology has impacted and will shape the individual and puble health and everyday life. My research has been focusing on vulnerability, social suffering, and the everyday struggles facing social minorities and the disadvantaged, particularly in relation to health policy and practice situated in the cultural and political economic context. I pay particular attention to individual access to health infrastructures amongst the underprivileged and marginalised communities for whom their everyday lives and metal health have been adversely impacted by the global pandemics, such as AIDS and COVID-19.
